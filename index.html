    What Do We Offer

    The word yoga comes from Sanscrit, the language of ancient India. It means union, integration or wholeness. It is an approach to health that promotes the harmonious collaboration of the human being’s three components: body, mind and spirit. While Yoga in Bandon honours the roots of yoga as a historic philosophical discipline, our weekly classes allow you to explore all spiritual, mental, emotional and physical impacts of yoga on body and mind, while increasing your overall fitness, flexibility and rejuvenation.

    See You On The Mat

    As anything in life, the benefits you will get from your yoga practice will be proportionate to the effort you put into it. Through regular engagement with Yoga in Bandon teaching, both online and in person, you will get to know your body, celebrate its incredible potential and commit to a life of self-care. You will organically integrate yoga into your life, seeing its practice and mindset not as something to be gotten over and done with, but rather as a pleasurable and self-deserving part of your day-to-day ritual.
